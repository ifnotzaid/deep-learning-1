## Responsibilities

### CNN Architecture Design
- Researched appropriate CNN architecture for CIFAR-10 at this course level
- Justified the 3-block design (doubling channels, halving spatial
  dimensions at each block) based on Week 3 depth vs width principles
- Documented why bias=False is correct when BatchNorm follows Conv layers

  to demonstrate the Week 3 principle that architecture matters more

- Researched the theoretical motivation for each optimizer in the
  SGD → Momentum → RMSProp → Adam progression
- Documented the canyon problem that motivates momentum

  unreliable gradient history at initialization
- Explained Adam's bias correction mechanism and why it matters for
  early training steps
- Connected the +6.6% Momentum improvement and +6.9% Adam improvement
  to their respective theoretical mechanisms
