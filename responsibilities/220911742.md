## What I Did

### Theoretical Grounding
Spent a lot of time on the Bayesian interpretation of regularization since
the professor emphasized this. L2 being a Gaussian prior and L1 being a
Laplace prior wasn't obvious at first but it actually explains everything
about why they behave differently.

Also worked out the early stopping ≈ L2 equivalence (α = 1/(τε)) which
ended up being one of the more interesting points in the report — it means
using both together is somewhat redundant which we didn't realize going in.
